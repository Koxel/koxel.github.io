About Koxel

Koxel is an open-world, adventure RPG, build from hexagonal tiles. Koxel is currently under heavy construction and won't be released anytime soon.

Koxel is set on an infinite world of hexagonal tiles, with a bunch of different biomes ranging from forests to poisonous swamps. Each location has its own fitting flora and fauna, that can be destroyed, harvested or be created as the player wishes.
